Our Story

Derani Jethani is a traditional Rajputi fashion boutique founded by Kalpana Kunwar in Udaipur. We specialize in Rajputi Poshaks and Jewelry for weddings, festivals, and royal celebrations.

With a legacy of trust and authentic craftsmanship, we bring elegance from the roots of Rajasthan to every doorstep across India.
