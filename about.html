About Us

Owner: Kalpana Kunwar

Derani Jethani is a homegrown boutique from Udaipur, Rajasthan, known for its authentic and elegant Rajputi Poshak and exquisite traditional jewelry.

We take pride in our quality, cultural heritage, and modern craftsmanship, serving customers across India with love, elegance, and royal designs.

Every piece is personally selected and curated to bring you a regal experience. We believe in trust, tradition, and timeless fashion.
